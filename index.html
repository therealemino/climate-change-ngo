Who We Are

Climate change is happening. I'ts one of the most serious environmental issue facing us, and humans are responsible for it. Sometimes misunderstood as being changes in the weather, in reality - climate change is about changes in our very own way of life.

Saving our planet, lifting people out of poverty, advancing economic growth... these are one and the same fight. Our aim is to connect the dots between climate change, water scarcity, energy shortages, global health, food security and women's empowerment. Solutions to one problem must be solutions for all.

Our Vision

Going Green! A environment free of pollution and excessive heat, economically advanced and poverty alleviated.

Our Mission

Connecting Dots. Finding long lasting solutions to critical global issues, climate change,poverty and empowerment

Our Values


Provide a more sustainable quality in dealings and embracing positive change.
